SUV

An SUV, or Sport Utility Vehicle, is a versatile vehicle type that combines elements of road-going passenger cars with features from off-road vehicles, like higher ground clearance and four-wheel drive. SUVs are known for their spacious interiors, making them ideal for families and outdoor adventures.

Sedan

A sedan is a type of passenger car that typically features a three-box configuration with separate compartments for the engine, passengers, and cargo. Sedans are characterized by their fixed B-pillars and usually have four doors. They are one of the most common car body styles, known for comfort and safety.

Limousine

A limousine is a luxury vehicle driven by a chauffeur, with a partition between the driver's compartment and the passenger's compartment. Limousines are often elongated for more spacious interiors and are associated with opulence and prestige. They are commonly used for special occasions, such as weddings and corporate events.

Roadster

A roadster is a convertible sports car designed for exhilarating performance and driving pleasure. Roadsters are typically two-seaters, have a soft top or a retractable hardtop, and focus on agility and fun driving dynamics. They are often associated with open-air motoring enjoyment.
